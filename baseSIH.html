Ecotourism & Natural Beauty

Explore the untouched natural beauty of Jharkhand's forests, waterfalls, and wildlife sanctuaries

Netarhat Forests

Explore the dense Sal forests of Netarhat, often called the 'Queen of Chotanagpur', with its rich biodiversity and breathtaking views.

Learn More

Hundru Falls

Witness the breathtaking Hundru Falls, one of the highest waterfalls in Jharkhand, where the Subarnarekha River drops from a height of 98 meters.

Learn More

Betla National Park

Discover the rich wildlife of Betla National Park, home to tigers, elephants, and numerous species of deer amidst pristine forest landscapes.

Learn More

Cultural Tapestry

Immerse yourself in the unique tribal traditions, folk music, and indigenous art forms of Jharkhand

Sarhul Festival

Experience the vibrant Sarhul festival, celebrated by tribal communities to mark the beginning of the new year and the spring season.

Learn More

Sohrai Art

Discover the ancient Sohrai painting tradition, a form of mural art created by indigenous women during harvest festivals.

Folk Music

Listen to the enchanting folk music of Jharkhand, featuring traditional instruments and songs that tell stories of nature and daily life.

Our Vision

Promoting sustainable tourism and preserving cultural heritage

At Discover Jharkhand, we are committed to promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities while preserving the natural beauty and cultural heritage of this magnificent region. We believe in creating meaningful travel experiences that foster understanding and appreciation for Jharkhand's unique traditions and ecosystems.

Our mission is to empower local communities through tourism, provide authentic experiences for travelers, and ensure that the wonders of Jharkhand can be enjoyed by generations to come.
